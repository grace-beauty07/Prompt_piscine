# Overview:
>- ## Before working with prompts and large language models, it's essential to understand the ethical side of AI.
>- ## This quest introduces the principles of responsible AI use: fairness, transparency, and accountability.
>- ## You'll learn how to identify bias, write safe prompts, and think critically about the social impact of your work.
---

<br>

# Learning Objectives:
>- ## Understand the basic principles of AI ethics and responsible use.
>- ## Identify common types of bias in datasets and model outputs.
>- ## Design prompts that are transparent, safe, and free from harmful assumptions.
>- ## Learn to handle sensitive or incorrect model outputs responsibly.
>- ## Understand the difference between using AI to learn vs. using AI to avoid learning.
>- ## Develop skills that make you irreplaceable as a coder.
