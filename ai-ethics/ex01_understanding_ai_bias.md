# Part A: Build Your Foundation (no AI first)

## Short Example Of Model Bias:
- ### An example of model bias occurs in beauty. For example when AI is prompted to "give a picture of a beautiful woman", it tends to lean towards a particular body type, face shape, eye shape, hair color etc.
## Why This Bias Exists:
- ### It exists as a result of datasets that reflects beautiful women as looking a specific way 
## Proposed mitigation (data balancing, prompt phrasing, model choice):
- ### Improving datasets balance by ensuring that training data has diverse examples of beautiful women

>## What happens when this bias appears in healthcare, hiring, or criminal justice?
>### My predictions:
>- ### In healthcare; some people are favoured more than others which might lead to high mortality rate; in hiring, some qualified, experienced profressionals might not get interviews; while in criminal justice some people may be unjustly detained with less chances of parole.
>### AI modifications:
>### Healthcare:
>- ### Imagine a hospital uses an AI system to decide which patients need extra care. The system learns from past data. But in the past, some groups of people received less medical attention or less spending on them. So the system learns that those groups “cost less” and assumes they are healthier.
>### Hiring:
>- ### In cases where systems learned from past hiring decisions. If a company mostly hired men before, the AI may learn that male-like resumes are “better.” It might downgrade resumes that include things like “women’s leadership club” because historically fewer women were hired.
>### Criminal Justice:
>- ### If the system was trained on data from a society where certain groups were policed more heavily, then those groups will appear to have more past arrests. The AI might then label them “high risk” more often.
