# Objective: Control tone and style with explicit instructions.
-----
# Task: "Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview"
-----
# Friendly prompt: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview using a friendly tone
# Output:
>## Dear [Candidate Name],
>## Thank you again for applying for the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. We really enjoyed reviewing your application and would love to invite you to interview with us.
>## We’re available on [Date] at [Time], and the interview will take place [via Zoom / at our office / by phone]. Please let us know if this time works for you, and we’ll be happy to send along the details. If it doesn’t, just let us know your availability and we’ll do our best to accommodate.
>## The conversation should take about [duration], and you’ll be meeting with [Interviewer Name(s)]. It will be a great chance for us to learn more about your experience and for you to ask any questions about the role or the team.
>## Looking forward to connecting with you soon!
>## Warm regards,
>## [Your Name]
>## [Your Job Title]
>## [Company Name]
>## [Contact Information]
-----
# Professional prompt: Write an email inviting a candidate to an interview using a professional tone
# Output:
>## Dear [Candidate Name],
>## Thank you for your application for the [Job Title] position at [Company Name]. After reviewing your qualifications, we would like to invite you to attend an interview.
>## We are available on [Date] at [Time], and the interview will be conducted [in person at our office / via Zoom / by phone]. Please confirm whether this time is convenient for you. If not, kindly share your availability, and we will do our best to accommodate.
>## The interview is expected to last approximately [duration], and you will be meeting with [Interviewer Name(s)]. This discussion will provide an opportunity to further explore your experience and discuss the role in greater detail.
>## We look forward to your response.
>## Sincerely,
>## [Your Name]
>## [Your Job Title]
>## [Company Name]
>## [Contact Information]
------
# Comparison
## The friendly tone is more relaxed while the professional tone is brisk and straight to the point.
