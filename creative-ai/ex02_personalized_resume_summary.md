# Objective: Apply prompting to professional use cases.

## Write a prompt that generates a resume summary based on role and skills.
---

<br>

## 1st Prompt:
>### Write a professional summary for a mechanical engineer in 5 sentences.
## Model output:
>### A dedicated Mechanical Engineer with strong expertise in the design, analysis, and optimization of mechanical systems. Experienced in applying engineering principles to develop efficient, reliable, and cost-effective solutions across diverse projects. Proficient in computer-aided design (CAD), simulation tools, and performance testing to ensure high standards of quality and safety. Skilled at collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to manage projects from concept through production and implementation. Committed to continuous improvement, innovation, and delivering engineering solutions that enhance operational performance and sustainability.
---
